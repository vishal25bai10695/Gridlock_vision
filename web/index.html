ABOUT PCII

Parking Congestion Impact Index (PCII) quantifies the traffic delay potential of illegal parking.

PCII = Wveh × Wviol × Wtime

  • Main Road/Double Parking blocks active lanes
  • Office commute peaks (AM/PM) amplify gridlock
